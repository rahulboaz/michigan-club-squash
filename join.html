Co-Ed Team
One unified co-ed squad open to all University of Michigan students — from beginners to experienced competitors. Founded in 2018, we compete in the College Squash Association (CSA) and welcome all skill levels.
  • CSA competition
  • Travel to regional & national tournaments
  • Coaching and structured practice
  • Tryouts held every September
  • All genders welcome

Not at all! We welcome all skill levels. While our A Team is highly competitive, we have spots for beginners who are eager to learn and improve. The tryout helps us place you on the right team.
