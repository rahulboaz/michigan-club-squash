Welcome to Michigan!

All undergraduate students, current or incoming, interested in playing squash at Michigan are encouraged to try out for the team in the fall. Unfortunately, due to the nature of limited court space and travel expenses, we can only take a certain number of players. To help us better understand your experience and skill-level, please fill out this short form.

Additionally, we encourage any prospective players, whether you are a rising junior in high school thinking about college squash or a senior in the process of deciding which college to attend next year, to fill out the form. Please email us at michiganclubsquash@gmail.com with any further questions.

We look forward to hearing from you and hope to see you on the courts. Feel free to explore our website and to sign up for our monthly newsletter to get to know our team better. Thank you and Go Blue!
