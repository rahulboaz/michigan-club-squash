The Hadley Recreation Center

Michigan Club Squash trains and competes at the brand new Hadley Recreation Center on the University of Michigan campus. Home to 2 state-of-the-art glass-back squash courts, the Hadley Center is one of the premier squash facilities in the Big Ten.

The facility serves as the team's full gym — members have access to the entire Hadley Center for training, conditioning, and recovery.

Courts
2 singles courts with glass back walls
